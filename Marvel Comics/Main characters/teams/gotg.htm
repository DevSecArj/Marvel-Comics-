Guardians of the Galaxy


Even the 31st Century needs heroes, and the call has been answered by 
the Guardians of the Galaxy, a team of superhuman and extraterrestrial 
adventurers dedicated to the safeguarding of the Milky Way Galaxy from 
any force that threatens the security or liberty of its various people.



A group of cosmic adventurers led by Star-Lord, the Guardians of the 
Galaxy protect the universe from threats all across space. The team also 
includes Drax, Gamora, Groot and Rocket Raccoon!
