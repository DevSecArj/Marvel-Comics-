The Avengers


The group began with the random teaming of Thor, Iron Man, Ant-Man, Wasp 
and Hulk, who joined forces to thwart the Asgardian menace Loki in 
response to a call for help from Hulk's teen sidekick, Rick Jones. Pym 
suggested the heroes remain together as a team, and his partner Wasp 
suggested they call themselves "something colorful and dramatic, 
like...the Avengers." The name stuck, and a legend was born.

Earth's Mightiest Heroes joined forces to take on threats that were too 
big for any one hero to tackle. With a roster that has included Captain 
America, Iron Man, Ant-Man, Hulk, Thor, Wasp and dozens more over the 
years, the Avengers have come to be regarded as Earth's No. 1 team.
