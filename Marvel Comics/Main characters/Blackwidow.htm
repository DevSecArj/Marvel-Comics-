Black Widow


Natasha Romanova, known by many aliases, is an expert spy, athlete, and 
assassin. Trained at a young age by the KGB's infamous Red Room Academy, the 
Black Widow was formerly an enemy to the Avengers. She later became their ally 
after breaking out of the U.S.S.R.'s grasp, and also serves as a top 
S.H.I.E.L.D. agent.
