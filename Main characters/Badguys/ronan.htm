Ronan


Born a Kree on the planet Hala, Ronan joined the Accuser Corps and 
quickly ascended to the top of the ranks. As The Supreme Accuser, Ronan 
enforces the laws of the Kree Empire throughout the galaxy and 
frequently clashes with Earth’s superheroes and other alien races.
